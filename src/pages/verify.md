If you need to confirm something really came from me, encrypt a message meant for my eyes only, or otherwise prove you're talking to the right person, this page collects the keys and proofs you need.

## GPG / PGP

My GnuPG public key is published at <https://gpg.l3x.in/>. Use it to verify signed messages or send me encrypted email.

## RSA

An RSA public key is also available at <https://rsa.l3x.in/> for situations where a plain RSA keypair is more convenient than a full OpenPGP key.

## Notes

- Both URLs return the raw key material over HTTPS — copy or download directly.
- Always verify the fingerprint through a separate, trusted channel if authenticity is critical. You can [contact me](/contact) to confirm a fingerprint out-of-band.
