@sir I strongly vouch for ProtonMail: I'm a free-tier user since ages and can't be any happier.

It's a rock solid service, both iOS and web apps are quite good and free tier storage is more than enough for my needs.

I recommend using email as a communication tool only (i.e. not as data storage), regardless of the service provider.

Also, I don't get the "open standard must be free" argument. Do you expect SMTP providers like Mailjet to give it away for free?
